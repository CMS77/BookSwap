Lend your books

Got books collecting dust on your shelf?
Give them a new life by sharing them with other readers. Simply add your books to your profile, and when someone shows interest, you can arrange a quick and easy handoff. Once they`re done, your book comes back — ready to be shared again.

Looking for your next great read?
Explore what others are sharing and discover hidden gems. When you find something you like, just send a request and connect with the owner. It's a simple and fun way to read more without buying more.

Meet & Exchange

Arrange a safe meeting in a public place and swap books in person.

See how it works →

When it's time to exchange, meet in a safe public place and swap books in person. It`s quick, friendly, and helps build a real community of readers. Confirm the exchange on the platform and keep the connection going for future swaps.
