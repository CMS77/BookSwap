How It Works

BookSwap is a community-driven platform that makes sharing and discovering books simple, free, and personal.

📚

List Your Books

Share books you've already read and give them a second life.

See how it works →
Put your shelf to work.
Add books to your profile and make them available to the community. When a reader shows interest, confirm the request and arrange a handoff. Your book finds a new reader — and returns when they're done.
🔍

Discover New Reads

Browse books shared by readers near you and find your next favourite.

See how it works →
Your next favourite is out there.
Browse the catalogue, search by title or author, and find something that catches your eye. Send a swap request to the owner and start the conversation. Reading more has never cost less.
🤝

Meet & Exchange

Arrange a safe, in-person handoff and swap books with real people.

See how it works →
Simple, safe, in person.
Agree on a public meeting spot, swap books face to face, and confirm the exchange on the platform. Rate your experience and keep the connection going for future swaps.

The process, step by step

1
Create your account Sign up for free and set up your profile in under a minute.
2
Add books to your profile List the books you own and are willing to share with the community.
3
Browse and request Explore available books and send a swap request to the one you want.
4
Confirm and arrange The owner reviews your request and you agree on a time and place to meet.
5
Meet and exchange Meet in a safe, public location and swap books in person.
6
Rate your experience Leave a rating after the exchange to help build a trusted community.
